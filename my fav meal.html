PASTA AND CHICKEN STEW

ABOUT

Contrary to a general belief that pasta originated from italy, it actually existed in china long before it became a thing in italy. Legend has it that Marco polo brought pasta to italy from china. Stew on the other has been in existence from ancient times and present in different parts of the world. There are different types of stew : Beef stew, Chicken stew, Fish stew, Lamb stew, e.t.c So I will be taking us through my favorite, which is the chicken stew and pasta.

INGREDIENTS

PASTA AND CHICKEN STEW
Pasta Water
8 drumsticks Goundnut oil
4 Bell peppers 1 Tin tomatoe
1tbs Curry 1tbs thyme
1 medium Onions 2pcs of Garlic
Fresh pepper Fresh tomatoes
Salt Seasoning cubes
Bay leaves Carrot

METHOD OF COOKING

  1. Pour 2 litres of water into a large pot and boil the pasta
  2. Add 1/2 tsp of salt and groundnut oil to taste
  3. Cook for 10mins and pour into a seive to drain the water
  4. Pour the chicken into a pot, add 1/2 tbs of curry,thyme, and half slice of onions, 2 cube of seasoning,salt and half cup of water and cover to boil
  5. Slice the fresh pepper, Onions, Tomatoes, Garlic and blend together
  6. Chop the bell peppers and carrot
  7. Take a new pot, place it on a low heat and add 5cl of groundnut oil
  8. Add a tin tomato into the oil and stir, add few slices of onions, the blended pepper and stir
  9. Then add the chopped bell peppers, 3 cubes of seasoning and leave to cook for 10mins
  10. Add the chicken drumsticks, stir properly and leave to cook for another 10mins
  11. Serve the stew with the cooked pasta, sprinkle some bay leaves and ENJOY !!!
